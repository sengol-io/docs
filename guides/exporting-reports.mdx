A risk assessment is only as useful as your ability to share and act on its findings. Whether you're presenting to your board, responding to an auditor's request, filing compliance documentation, or populating an internal risk registry, Sengol lets you export your full assessment results in formats designed for each of those audiences.

## Export formats

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="PDF Report" icon="file-pdf">
    A professionally formatted document containing your risk scores, findings, mitigation recommendations, evidence, and compliance mapping. Ideal for **auditors, executives, legal teams, and board presentations** — it's designed to be shared as-is, without further editing.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Excel Export" icon="file-excel">
    A structured spreadsheet with all assessment data in tabular form. Ideal for **risk registries, internal analysis, and further data processing** — your risk and compliance teams can filter, sort, and integrate the data into existing workflows.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Exporting a report

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the assessment you want to export">
    Complete a risk assessment, or navigate to an existing one from your project history in the [dashboard](https://sengol.ai/dashboard). Open the assessment to view its full results page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Export">
    In the top-right corner of the results page, click the **Export** button. This opens the export options panel.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose your format">
    Select either **PDF Report** or **Excel Export** depending on your audience and intended use. You can export both formats from the same assessment if needed.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Download the file">
    The file generates and downloads automatically to your device. PDF reports may take 30–60 seconds for comprehensive assessments — see the note below.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What's included in the PDF report

The PDF report is structured for readability by non-technical stakeholders as well as technical reviewers. It includes:

- **Executive summary** — your overall risk score, a plain-language interpretation of the score band, and a high-level summary of the most critical findings
- **Category breakdown** — scores and narrative summaries for Performance, Security, Compliance, and Operational Risk
- **Top 5 risks** — each risk listed with its severity rating, a description of the finding, and the specific recommended mitigation action
- **Cost estimates for mitigation** — approximate cost ranges for each mitigation, supporting budget conversations and prioritization
- **Evidence-based insights** — the top matching real-world AI incidents with incident ID, company, date, failure type, match factors, and relevance score
- **Compliance framework mapping** — a view of which regulatory frameworks your system addresses and where gaps exist
- **Assessment metadata** — date of assessment, the assessor's name, and the project it belongs to, providing a traceable audit record

<Tip>
  The PDF report is **audit-ready** and can be provided directly to compliance officers, external auditors, or board members without modification.
</Tip>

<Note>
  Report generation may take **30–60 seconds** for comprehensive assessments. Assessments covering multiple jurisdictions, a large number of risk factors, or extensive evidence matches will take longer to render. Do not close the tab while the report is generating.
</Note>
