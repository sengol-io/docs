Before you greenlight an AI investment, Sengol gives you a quantitative, evidence-based picture of what could go wrong: performance failures, security exposures, compliance gaps, and operational blind spots, all scored on a 0–100 scale and mapped to the frameworks your auditors already know.

## How it works

Sengol turns a structured questionnaire about your AI system into a prioritized risk report in three steps — no consultants, no long procurement cycles, and no post-deployment surprises.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create your account">
    Sign up at [sengol.ai/auth/register](https://sengol.ai/auth/register) — the Free plan is available immediately with no credit card. Paid plans include a 14-day free trial.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Run an assessment">
    Navigate to your [dashboard](https://sengol.ai/dashboard) and start a new risk assessment. Answer questions across 15+ categories covering your AI model type, data sensitivity, security posture, compliance requirements, and more. Most assessments take 5–20 minutes.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Get scores and recommendations">
    Receive an overall risk score (0–100) plus a breakdown across Performance, Security, Compliance, and Operational dimensions. Review your top risks, mapped compliance gaps, and actionable mitigation recommendations — ready to share with your team or export for audit.
  </Step>
</Steps>
