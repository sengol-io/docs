The Sengol Compliance Dashboard gives your team and leadership a single, continuously updated view of your AI governance posture. Instead of assembling compliance evidence manually from spreadsheets and assessment PDFs, the dashboard aggregates your risk scores, framework alignment status, and gap analysis into structured panels designed for both technical teams and executive audiences.

## Dashboard overview

The Compliance Dashboard is organized into four primary panels, each targeting a different level of analysis.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Risk Score Trending" icon="chart-line">
    Visualizes how your overall and dimension-level risk scores change over time. Track the impact of mitigation actions, infrastructure changes, and new assessments against a historical baseline.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Compliance Gap Analysis" icon="list-check">
    Breaks down your compliance posture by framework. For each applicable standard, the dashboard identifies which requirements you satisfy, which are partially met, and which have open gaps requiring action.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Industry Benchmarking" icon="chart-bar">
    Compares your risk scores against anonymized data from similar organizations in your industry. See where you lead, where you lag, and where the highest-impact improvements lie relative to your peer group.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Board-Level Summary" icon="presentation-screen">
    Produces a concise, exportable summary of your AI governance status — overall risk posture, top open gaps, trend direction, and compliance coverage — formatted for executives, board members, and auditors.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Supported compliance frameworks

Sengol maps your assessment results to the requirements of the following frameworks automatically. You do not need to manually tag controls or cross-reference requirements.

| Framework | Scope |
|---|---|
| **EU AI Act** | EU regulation on high-risk AI systems |
| **GDPR** | EU data protection and privacy |
| **CCPA** | California consumer privacy |
| **HIPAA** | US health data privacy and security |
| **SOC 2** | Service organization security controls |
| **ISO 27001** | Information security management systems |
| **ISO 42001** | AI management systems |
| **PCI-DSS** | Payment card data security |
| **NIST AI RMF** | AI risk management framework (US) |

Sengol updates framework mappings as regulations evolve. When guidance changes — such as new EU AI Act implementing acts or NIST AI RMF profile updates — your compliance status reflects the latest requirements automatically.

## Accessing the compliance dashboard

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in to your Sengol account">
    Sign in at [https://sengol.ai/dashboard](https://sengol.ai/dashboard). The Compliance Dashboard is available on Pro plan and above.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open the Compliance Dashboard">
    Navigate to **Tools → Compliance Dashboard** in the sidebar, or go directly to [https://sengol.ai/tools/compliance/dashboard](https://sengol.ai/tools/compliance/dashboard).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select your project or portfolio">
    Choose a single project to review compliance for one AI system, or select multiple projects to view a portfolio-level summary across all your AI initiatives.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review your compliance status and download reports">
    Explore the dashboard panels to analyze gaps, review trend data, and benchmark against your industry. Use the **Export** button to download the board-level summary as a PDF for sharing with stakeholders.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  The Compliance Dashboard is available on the **Pro plan and above**. Free plan users can view overall risk scores in the main dashboard, but do not have access to gap analysis, framework mapping, benchmarking, or the board-level summary export.
</Note>

<Tip>
  Use the board-level summary export to share AI governance status with executives and auditors. The exported report is formatted for non-technical audiences and includes trend direction, top open gaps, and a plain-language compliance coverage summary — no additional editing required.
</Tip>
