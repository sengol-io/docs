A Sengol project is a persistent workspace for a single AI system or initiative. It brings together every risk assessment you have run, your compliance documentation, relevant regulatory signals, and the mitigation plans your team is executing — all in one place, with a full history so you can demonstrate improvement over time and respond to audits without scrambling to reconstruct records.

## What a project contains

Each project acts as a living record of your AI system's risk and compliance posture.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Risk Assessment History" icon="clock-rotate-left">
    Every risk assessment saved to the project is stored with its full score breakdown and timestamp. Review how your Performance, Security, Compliance, and Operational scores have moved over time as you make changes to your system or implement controls.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Compliance Documentation" icon="file-shield">
    Store framework mappings, evidence artifacts, and compliance notes alongside your assessments. The project links your compliance status directly to the assessment results that support it, reducing duplication and manual cross-referencing.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Regulatory Signals" icon="signal">
    Regulatory signals relevant to your project's domain — new guidance, enforcement actions, framework updates — are surfaced automatically based on the industry and jurisdictions associated with the project.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Mitigation Plans" icon="list-check">
    Track the recommendations generated by each assessment. Mark items in progress, assign owners, and record completion. Mitigation progress is reflected in your trend data so stakeholders can see the direct impact of remediation work.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

</Note>

## Creating and managing projects

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Projects section">
    Go to [https://sengol.ai/dashboard](https://sengol.ai/dashboard) and select **Projects** from the sidebar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create a new project">
    Click **New Project** and enter a project name and a brief description of the AI system it represents. This description helps Sengol surface relevant regulatory signals and match incident data to your project context.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Run and save risk assessments">
    Navigate to the Risk Assessment tool and run an assessment for this AI system. When you submit your results, select the project you want to save it to. The assessment is added to the project's history immediately.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Track mitigation progress and review score history">
    Return to the project at any time to review your score trend, update mitigation item statuses, and run follow-up assessments. Each new assessment adds a data point to your history, letting you demonstrate a clear trajectory of improvement to auditors and stakeholders.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Team collaboration

On the **Team plan and above**, up to 10 users in your organization can access the same project simultaneously. Shared access means that:

- All team members can **view assessments** saved to the project, without each person needing to run their own
- Compliance and security leads can **review evidence** and update mitigation status without needing to re-export and share files manually
- The project provides a **single source of truth** for your team's AI risk and compliance posture, reducing version confusion and coordination overhead

Team-level compliance status is visible at the portfolio level in the Compliance Dashboard, giving leadership a consolidated view across all active projects.

For more detail on inviting team members, setting permissions, and working collaboratively inside a project, see the [Team Collaboration](/guides/team-collaboration) guide.
