## Frequently asked questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Can I start with Free and upgrade later?">
    Yes. You can upgrade from Free to Pro, Team, or Enterprise at any time from your account settings. All of your existing assessments, projects, and saved data move with you — nothing is lost when you upgrade.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What's included in the 14-day free trial?">
    The free trial gives you full access to every feature in the plan you selected — including PDF and Excel export on Pro, team collaboration on Team, and API access on Enterprise. No credit card is required to start. If you decide not to continue, your account automatically reverts to the Free plan at the end of the trial period.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How does API access work?">
    The Enterprise plan includes REST API access at 2,000 requests per hour as a standard feature. If you are on a Pro or Team plan and need API access, you can add the Growth API (+$199/mo, 500 req/hour) or Enterprise API (+$499/mo, 2,000 req/hour) add-on. See the [API Reference](/api/overview) for full documentation and authentication details.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I change plans at any time?">
    Yes. Upgrades take effect immediately and are prorated for the remainder of your billing period. Downgrades take effect at the end of your current billing period, so you keep access to your current plan's features until the period closes.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

<Note>
  All paid plans include a **14-day free trial**. No credit card is required to start — you will only be charged if you choose to continue after the trial period ends.
</Note>
